Credits are VibeScale's currency. They are consumed when running analyses and using platform features. You can buy them in packages or earn them through the referral program.

## Your balance

The **Credits Wallet** screen displays the **Total balance** with a visual breakdown:

- **Purchased** — credits acquired via package
- **Bonus** — credits earned through referrals or promotions

<Note>
  Bonus credits are consumed first. Your purchased credits are only debited after the bonus balance runs out.
</Note>

## Referral program — Refer and Earn

Refer friends and earn **30 credits** for each friend who creates an app on VibeScale.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate your link">
    Click **Generate my referral link**. Your unique link is created instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share it">
    Copy the link to send wherever you prefer, or use the email field to send it directly through the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your referrals">
    The **Your Referrals** section lists each person you referred, with email, date, and status:
    - **Pending** — the friend hasn't created an app yet
    - **+30 ✓** — credits credited, qualified referral
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  A referral qualifies when the invited friend creates and connects an app on the platform. Just inviting is not enough.
</Tip>

## Transaction history

The **History** section records all movements in your balance:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Icon | Green for credits in, red for credits out |
| Description | Source of the transaction (purchase, analysis, referral bonus, etc.) |
| Date and time | When the transaction occurred |
| Amount | Credits added (+) or consumed (−) |
| Balance after | Resulting balance after the transaction |
