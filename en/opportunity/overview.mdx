The **Opportunity Scale Review (OSR)** is VibeScale's market validation pipeline. The platform automatically analyzes your GitHub repository, identifies what your app solves, who it solves it for, and how it positions against the competition — and turns all of this into an actionable strategic report.

No lengthy forms, no pitch decks to fill out. VibeScale reads your code and infers the product context. You confirm what's right, correct what isn't, and receive an opportunity analysis calibrated to your app's reality.

## The three steps

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Discovery" icon="magnifying-glass" href="/en/opportunity/discovery">
    The platform analyzes your repository and displays a panel with more than 8 automatically inferred fields: category, target audience, differentiator, competitors, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Alignment" icon="check-circle" href="/en/opportunity/alignment">
    You review and confirm the inferred data. The final report is built from your answers — not the platform's assumptions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Report" icon="file-chart-bar" href="/en/opportunity/report">
    Visual report with scores per dimension, positive signals, identified risks, and up to three priority actions to scale your app.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Track progress on the panel

The panel displays a progress bar with the three steps and each one's status:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ✓ Completed | Step finished successfully |
| ⏳ In Progress | Analysis running |
| ○ Pending | Waiting for previous steps |
| ✗ Failed | An error occurred — try again |

The steps are sequential: Alignment is only unlocked after Discovery, and the Report is only generated after Alignment is confirmed.
