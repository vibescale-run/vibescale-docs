The Opportunity Report consolidates everything analyzed and confirmed in the previous steps into a visual strategic analysis — with scores, risks, positive signals, and concrete actions.

## What the report contains

### Analysis by dimension

The report evaluates your app across multiple market and product dimensions. Each dimension receives a score reflecting the strength of the opportunity on that axis, based on the data confirmed during Alignment.

### Positive signals

A list of favorable factors identified in the analysis — competitive differentiators, clarity of value proposition, addressable market size, and other points working in the app's favor.

### Identified risks

Areas of concern that could compromise product traction or growth. These risks are not verdicts — they are signals to anticipate problems before they become blockers.

### Priority actions

Up to three concrete actions, ordered by impact, that you can take to increase the app's potential. Each action is specific to your product's context — not generic advice.

## How to interpret scores

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="High score in a dimension">
    Indicates the app has solid fundamentals in that area. Use it as confirmation that you're on the right track — and as supporting evidence in conversations with investors or partners.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Low score in a dimension">
    Indicates a gap worth addressing. Read the priority actions associated with that dimension to understand what could improve the result.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Dimensions with mid-range score">
    These offer the greatest leverage. Focused effort on them tends to move the overall score more than any other area. Prioritize them in your next product iterations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to re-run the analysis

After implementing product improvements or adjusting your positioning, you can generate a new report to compare progress.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete the security check">
    The **Run again** button is only unlocked after completing at least one [security check](/en/security/overview) cycle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Run again'">
    On the panel, click **Run again** to restart the full pipeline — Discovery, Alignment, and Report — with the current repository data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Alignment again">
    The new run goes through Alignment again. Take the opportunity to update any field that has changed since the last analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Each run generates an independent new report. The history of previous reports is not overwritten — you can compare your app's potential over time.
</Note>

## Natural next step: security check

A validated market opportunity is worth more when the product is technically trustworthy. The recommended next step is to start the [security check](/en/security/overview).

<Tip>
  Investors and technical partners increasingly ask for security evidence, not just traction. Having a clean security report is a concrete differentiator in due diligence processes.
</Tip>
