The Opportunity Report consolidates everything analyzed and confirmed in previous stages into a strategic visual analysis — with scores, risks, positive signals, and concrete actions.

## What the Report Contains

### Dimension Analysis

The report evaluates your app across multiple market and product dimensions. Each dimension receives a score reflecting the strength of the opportunity in that area, based on data confirmed during Alignment.

### Positive Signals

A list of favorable factors identified in the analysis — competitive advantages, clear value proposition, addressable market size, and other points working in the app’s favor.

### Identified Risks

Areas of concern that could hinder traction or product growth. These risks are not verdicts — they are early warnings to anticipate problems before they become blockers.

### Priority Actions

Up to three concrete actions, ranked by impact, that you can take to increase your app’s potential. Each action is tailored to your product’s context — not generic advice.

## How to Interpret Scores

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="High score in a dimension">
    Indicates the app has solid fundamentals in that area. Use this as confirmation you’re on the right track — and as a talking point with investors or partners.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Low score in a dimension">
    Indicates a gap worth addressing. Review the priority actions linked to that dimension to understand what can improve the score.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Medium scores in dimensions">
    These offer the greatest leverage. Focused effort here tends to move the overall score more than any other area. Prioritize these in your next product iterations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Re-run the Analysis

After implementing product improvements or adjusting your positioning, you can generate a new report to compare progress.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete the security scan">
    The **Run Again** button is only enabled after completing at least one cycle of the [security scan](/en/security/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Run Again'">
    In the dashboard, click **Run Again** to restart the full pipeline — Discovery, Alignment, and Report — with the current repository data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Alignment again">
    The new run goes through Alignment again. Take this opportunity to update any fields that have changed since the last analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Each run generates a new independent report. Previous reports are not overwritten — you can track your app’s potential evolution over time.
</Note>

## Natural Next Step: Security Scan

A validated market opportunity is more valuable when the product is technically reliable. The recommended next step is to start the [security scan](/en/security/overview).

<Tip>
  Investors and technical partners increasingly demand security evidence, not just traction. Having a clean security report is a concrete advantage during due diligence.
</Tip>
