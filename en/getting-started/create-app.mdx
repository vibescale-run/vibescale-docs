---
title: "Create an App"
description: "How to add your app to VibeScale and set up the basic information."
icon: "plus"
---

Each app in VibeScale represents a real product you want to analyze. The creation process takes less than two minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open My Apps">
    Log in at [vibescale.run](https://vibescale.run). On the home screen, click **Add App**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter name and description">
    Fill in the app name and a clear description of what it does and who it’s for. The more precise the description, the more relevant the opportunity analysis generated will be.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate the production URL">
    Provide the URL where the app is published and accessible. The platform automatically checks if the URL responds before completing the registration. Make sure the app is live before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm creation">
    Click **Create App**. Once done, you will see the notification "App created successfully!" and be redirected to the new app’s dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Each account can have up to **3 apps** simultaneously. If you need to add a new app beyond this limit, delete one of the existing apps first on the **My Apps** screen.
</Note>
