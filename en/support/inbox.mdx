The **Inbox** is VibeScale's official support channel. Use it to ask questions, report issues, or request help with any platform feature.

## Creating a ticket

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Ticket">
    The **New Ticket** button is at the top corner of the support screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the form">
    - **Ticket subject** — a clear summary of what you need
    - **Related app** — optional, select the app if the question is specific to one
    - **Description** — describe your question or issue in detail
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the ticket">
    Click **Send**. You will see the confirmation: "Support ticket created! Our team has been notified."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tracking the ticket

After creation, the ticket opens as a **chat-style thread**. You can:

- Read team responses directly in the thread
- Add new messages while the ticket is open
- Track the status at the top of the conversation

## Ticket status

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Open">
    The ticket was created and is waiting for a response. Our team has been notified and will review it shortly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Awaiting support">
    The ticket has been escalated for human attention. A team member is reviewing the case.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Resolved">
    The request has been closed. If the issue comes back, open a new ticket.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  All communication is recorded in the ticket thread. There is no need to send a separate email.
</Note>
